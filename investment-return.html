FUTURE PLANNING - FUTURE TOWN OF BANGLADESH

1. Introduction

Future Town of Bangladesh is an exceptional real estate company. In this institution, the opportunity of investing in real estate companies has been created for people of all classes from low to high income. Future Town of Bangladesh has taken multifaceted steps to show the common people the way of safe housing and community through multi-faceted investment.

2. Purpose

Interest-free living and interest-free investment schemes. All our activities will be interest-free, that is our commitment. As a Muslim, you have an imperative duty to keep yourself interest-free. All projects under this organization will be managed on a share basis. We have created the opportunity to fulfill your investment wishes in the first taste. People with low income, who have taste but not much capital, will also get an opportunity to invest easily here. It is our hope that your investment is safe and profitable.

3. Mission and Vision

Our strong commitment is to earn halal and live an interest-free life through interest-free investments. In this regard, our main goal is to make everyone interested in interest-free investment. I believe that by living an interest-free life, Allah Ta'ala will bless our business, family, and society, Inshallah.

4. Planning

This organization is planning to build condominium cities with modern civic facilities for implementation of infrastructure development plans at different district/upazila levels of Bangladesh. Projects will be implemented at the institutional vs individual level under Partnership Project Development (PPD).

5. Providing Modern Civic Amenities

Dream housing with modern civic amenities will be constructed considering the purchasing power of low-income people at each district/upazila level. Facilities will include: (a) Easy transportation (b) Educational institutions (c) Mega super shop (d) Swimming pool (e) Tailor shop (f) Laundry shop (g) Medicine corner (h) Technician (i) Security service (j) Community Center (k) Children's Park (l) Walkways (m) Mosque.

6. Investment Sector

Future Town of Bangladesh has investment opportunities in seven sectors, namely: (a) Company shares (b) Investment Package (c) Yearly Investment (d) Company Founder Members (e) 70% payment then registry facilities (f) Share of land (g) Plot purchase Installment.

7. Investment Money

All the money invested in favor of this institution will be invested in various projects. No cash will be kept out of investment in the company, so investors' money will be fully protected. We are committed to implementing this promise.

8. Area of Investment

Future Town of Bangladesh has undertaken a multifaceted program to facilitate the interest-free investment system. To bring this system within reach, activities will soon expand to different district/upazila levels throughout Bangladesh.

9. Investment System

Each project will be implemented on a share basis. The initial price of each share for all projects will be fixed at Tk 100. A subscriber can purchase a minimum of 100 shares, with the maximum not exceeding 49% of the project value. The share price will be recalculated every 3 months, and dividends will be declared at the end of the year.

10. Duration of Investment

Shares of different tenures can be purchased, ranging from 1 to 10 years. Short-term (6-9 months) shares will be available from January 2025. Notifications for share sales must be given at least 2 months in advance for long-term shares and 1 month in advance for short-term shares.

11. Invest Online

Invest in various packages such as Company shares, Investment Packages, and more from anywhere through our website. Shareholders will be provided with individual login credentials to access investment updates.

12. To Create Employment for Unemployed People

We will soon take the initiative to create employment in various district/upazila levels. Educated and uneducated unemployed youth will be able to do commission-based work or get jobs through investing in our projects. Professional people will also be employed in various sectors, ensuring income generation.

13. Transparency in Financial Management

Our commitment is to maintain complete transparency in all financial transactions and management processes. Shareholders will have access to detailed financial reports and updates regarding their investments.

14. Encouraging Entrepreneurship

Future Town of Bangladesh aims to encourage entrepreneurship by providing opportunities for business partnerships and investments in various projects. This initiative will support budding entrepreneurs in establishing successful ventures.

15. Commitment to Social Welfare

We are dedicated to contributing to social welfare by undertaking various community development programs. Our projects aim to improve the living standards of people in different districts and upazilas across Bangladesh.

16. Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

As part of our CSR initiatives, we will engage in various philanthropic activities, including education, healthcare, and environmental conservation projects, to make a positive impact on society.

17. Environmental Sustainability

Our projects will be developed with a focus on environmental sustainability, implementing eco-friendly practices and green technologies to minimize our ecological footprint.

18. Continuous Improvement and Innovation

We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation in our projects and services. By embracing new technologies and best practices, we aim to provide the highest quality living and investment opportunities for our stakeholders.
